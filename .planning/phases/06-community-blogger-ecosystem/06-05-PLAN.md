1. `getProducts(params)`: GET /blogger/products

7. `getDashboard(period)`: GET /blogger/dashboard?period=7d|30d
8. `getTrendData(metric, period)`: GET /blogger/dashboard/trend/:metric?period=7d|30d
9. `getBloggerProducts(bloggerId)`: GET /blogger/users/:id/products

- `fetchDashboard(period)`: 获取面板数据
- `fetchTrendData(metric, period)`: 获取趋势数据
- `fetchProducts()`: 获取商品列表
