## 6. Screenshot Strategy

Screenshots are the #1 conversion factor after the icon.

| Screenshot | Content | Text Overlay |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| 1 (Hero) | AI Stylist outfit plan view | "AI Stylist - Your Personal Fashion Consultant" |
| 2 | Virtual try-on before/after | "Virtual Try-On - See It Before You Buy" |
| 3 | Recommendation waterfall feed | "Smart Feed - Curated Just For You" |
| 4 | Style quiz visual cards | "Style Quiz - Discover Your Style DNA" |
| 5 | Product detail with size rec | "Smart Shopping - Perfect Fit Every Time" |
| 6 | Color season palette result | "Color Analysis - Know Your Best Colors" |

### Screenshot Best Practices
- Use real app UI, not mockups
- Add short, benefit-oriented text overlay in Chinese
- Use consistent branding (colors, fonts)
- First 3 screenshots are most important (visible without scrolling)
- Bright, high-contrast design for thumbnail visibility

## 7. Category Selection

### App Store
- **Primary:** Lifestyle (broader reach, less competition than Shopping)
- **Secondary:** Shopping (captures shopping intent)

### Google Play
- **Category:** Lifestyle
- **Tags:** Fashion, AI, Shopping, Style

## 8. Rating & Review Strategy

- Target 4.5+ star rating at launch
- Implement in-app rating prompt after positive experiences (completed purchase, successful try-on)
- Use SKStoreReviewController (iOS) / In-App Review API (Android) for system prompts
- Respond to all negative reviews within 24 hours
- Common negative themes to watch: slow AI response, try-on quality, payment issues
